# Contributing and Feedback

For an overview of the QEEPP framework, start here:  www.qeepp.com

---

QEEPP is an authored framework, and its core conceptual structure remains under the author’s direction.

Thoughtful feedback, practical critique, implementation suggestions, and industry-specific use cases are welcome to help refine its application and improve its usefulness across real-world transformation environments.

## How to contribute

You can contribute in any of these ways:

- **Share an industry use case**: a brief scenario, context, constraints, and what worked or failed at scale.
- **Suggest refinements**: wording improvements, missing concepts, edge cases, or clearer definitions.
- **Provide implementation feedback**: how you applied the model, what governance cadence you used, and what evidence was most useful.

## Where to send feedback

- **Website contact form**: [QEEPP](https://qeepp.com/#contact) (Talk to us)
- **Email**: contact@qeepp.com

## What to include (recommended)

When sharing a case or suggestion, please include:

- Industry and context (1–2 sentences)
- Transformation goal (what was being modernized)
- Scale/constraints (regulatory, data, security, uptime, cost)
- Where maturity broke down (Q, E, E, P, or P)
- What you would change if doing it again

Thank you for helping pressure-test QEEPP in real-world environments.
