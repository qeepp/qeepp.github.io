QEEPP Full Diagnostic Assessment

Evidence-based structural assessment for deeper QEEPP diagnostics

This full diagnostic version converts the QEEPP practical framework into a guided evidence-capture workflow across all 25 structural controls.

Instead of assigning direct maturity scores control by control, the assessor responds to three diagnostic questions for each control, records supporting notes, and lets the scoring engine derive the maturity score.

How this version differs from the free assessment

  • Three guided diagnostic questions for each of the 25 controls
  • Help text for what to check before choosing an answer
  • Evidence notes saved for later review and interpretation
  • Derived control scoring instead of direct self-assigned maturity
  • Warnings when high scores are not supported by evidence quality

Assessment setup

Capture context first. This helps preserve assessment scope, make saved sessions more useful, and support more reliable interpretation later.

Assessment guidance

Use the guided prompts to estimate the maturity of each control from evidence, not instinct.

Recommended evidence logic

For each control, review the three question types in order:

Existence → Consistency → Operational reality

High maturity should not be claimed when evidence is assumed, weak, or incomplete.

What gets saved

  • Selected answers for all three prompts per control
  • Evidence notes, basis, and confidence
  • Derived control scores and dimension scores
  • Dependency warnings and overall diagnostic profile
