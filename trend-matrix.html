QEEPP Trends

A visual model used to track repeated QEEPP assessments over time across Quality, Effectiveness, Efficiency, Performance, and Productivity.

Each matrix represents one assessment period. Each dimension fills vertically from level 1 to level 5, making trend movement and structural balance easy to compare.
