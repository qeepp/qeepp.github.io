QEEPP Starfish Diagram

QEEPP Startfish represents the interconnectedness of the five dimentions.

It symbolizes awarenes, guidance, protection, resilience, adaptability, balance, and structural strength.



Starfish symbolize

Awareness and perception Starfish have a unique anatomy, which allows them to sense their environment, and react quickly to changes
Guidance and protection Islanders believed that starfish protects sailors from danger while guiding and escorting navigating unfamiliar waters
Renewal and regeneration Starfish have a complex nervous system and anatomy which enables them to regrow lost elements and restore symetry
Strength and resilience Starfish can thrive in challenging situations and adapt to ever-changing environments
Balance and harmony Starfish’s unique symmetry and regenerating ability represent the interconnectedness of all elements


QEEPP Starfish Diagram Zones
Governance Zone  •  Is the transformation aligned and monitored Ambition Zone  •  Is the transformation optimized for scaling Stability Zone  •  Is the transformation structured and stabilized
The outer starfish represents the ideal shape which is the goal for a fully structured transformation
The inner shape illustartes the QEEPP assesment of the current state. Irregular shapes signal danger, small shapes signal immaturity.
