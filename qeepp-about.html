QEEPP | Quality – Effectiveness – Efficiency – Performance – Productivity

Authorship

QEEPP (pronounced "cue-eep") was conceived and developed by Rado Dimitrov as a structural discipline framework for enterprise-scale digital transformation.


The framework reflects practical experience in cloud architecture, enterprise modernization, AI platform governance, and operating model optimization.
It was designed to address a recurring systemic gap in large-scale transformation programs: the tendency to scale initiatives before stabilizing foundational quality, aligning to measurable outcomes, and institutionalizing performance discipline.


QEEPP's structured progression Stabilize→Align→Optimize→Measure→Scale helps ensure that transformation efforts do not amplify instability, inefficiency, or strategic misalignment.
It enables organizations to stabilize Quality, align Effectiveness, optimize Efficiency, measure Performance, and scale Productivity.


The architecture, articulation, implementation guidance, assessment tools, scoring logic, questionnaires, and software originate from the author.
